My Top Five Sega Franchises

  I am a gamer. I like a variety of different types of games. And for me, there is one company that always has a game to fit my mood. No matter if I want just a quick ten-minute gaming session or sit down for five-hours immersing myself in an emotional heartfelt story. That company is SEGA. And to honor this company and the developers who devoted their time and effort into making these games, I have made this website that overviews my top five SEGA franchises.
